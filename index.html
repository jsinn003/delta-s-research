About
Who we are
Delta S Research is an investment research team founded by a group of researchers across Singapore, London, and Hong Kong. With academic roots at the London School of Economics and Nanyang Technological University, we focus on both quantitative research and active portfolio management, exploring how market entropy shapes the decisions of investors.
What we do
We examine how shifts in market entropy shape investor behavior and drive price formation. Our research is not purely theoretical or abstract, but is designed to center around execution.
Our approach
We start with careful, ground-up research and turn it into algorithmic ways of reading the market. Options strategies are the core of how we operate, and our algorithms help interpret signals and guide decisions. We actively manage our assets to effectively navigate volatile markets.
Audience
Our work is for investors, capital allocators, and researchers who reject the assumption of stable equilibrium. We focus on those who view markets as changing systems. We believe that understanding disorder, rather than assuming stability, provides a more reliable basis for strategy.
