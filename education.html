The Approach

Most options instruction starts at the wrong end. It begins with strategies, then attempts to back-fill the structure underneath them.

We invert the order. Foundations first, then the Greeks, then the volatility surface, and only then the trades that exploit it.

Each lesson closes a single concept. Read in sequence or out of order, the structure holds.
